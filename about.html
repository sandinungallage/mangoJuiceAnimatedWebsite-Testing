Born on an
Extraordinary Island

Sri Lanka, the pearl of the Indian Ocean, is a tropical paradise where ancient heritage, rich volcanic soil, and warm hospitality come together to craft nature's finest bounties.

The Soil, The Sun, The Rain

Our ingredients are grown on smallholder family-owned agricultural estates throughout the island. The unique micro-climates of Sri Lanka—ranging from the humid coastal groves where golden King Coconuts thrive, to the misty highland valleys where aromatic passion fruits ripen—endow our produce with a sweetness and nutrient density that cannot be replicated anywhere else.
