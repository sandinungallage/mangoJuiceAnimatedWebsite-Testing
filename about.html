Born on an

Sri Lanka — the Pearl of the Indian Ocean — is a tropical paradise where ancient agricultural heritage, rich volcanic soils, and the world's most generous sunshine conspire to grow nature's finest fruits.

The Soil, The Sun, The Rain

Every Island Squeeze juice begins with a specific place. Our ingredients are grown by smallholder families across Sri Lanka's extraordinary range of micro-climates — from the humid coastal groves where King Coconuts flourish under Indian Ocean salt air, to the cool highland valleys where aromatic passion fruits ripen slowly in mist and mineral-rich red volcanic soil. The diversity of this island, in the space of a few hundred kilometres, produces flavour complexity that cannot be replicated anywhere else on earth.

The Landscapes That Shape Our Flavors

Each geographical region of Sri Lanka contributes a distinct soil type, altitude, and microclimate that directly shapes the character of the fruits we press.

UNESCO World Heritage

Sigiriya & The Dry Zone

The intense heat and semi-arid soils of the North Central Province concentrate extraordinary natural sugar levels in our mango and pomegranate groves.

Hill Country

Ella & the Uva Highlands

Misty mountain terraces above 1,000m produce our passion fruit. The dramatic day-to-night temperature swings create a tart complexity no lowland orchard can match.

Coastal Heritage

Galle & the Southern Coast

The sandy coastal soils and warm Indian Ocean breezes south of Galle Fort nurture our King Coconut groves — delivering pure Thambili electrolyte water.

Tea & Produce Country

Nuwara Eliya Highlands

At 1,868m — Sri Lanka's highest town — the cool, mist-laden air and iron-red volcanic soil produce intensely aromatic seasonal botanicals for our limited drops.

Tropical Coastline

Mirissa & South Coast

Crescent beaches of golden sand border our coastal coconut and pineapple estates, where the proximity to the ocean adds a subtle mineral depth to the fruit.

East Coast

Arugam Bay & the East

The drier eastern province contributes wild Wood Apple (Divul) and seasonal lime from sun-baked small-plot farms maintained by multi-generational families.

Our Farmers

47 Families.
One Island.

Island Squeeze works with 47 smallholder farming families spread across Sri Lanka's nine agricultural provinces. Every farm is visited personally. Every crop is tested individually. We pay a minimum of 40% above market rate — because exceptional fruit requires exceptional care, and the people who grow it deserve to know their work is valued.
