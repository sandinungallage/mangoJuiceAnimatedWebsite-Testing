Quick Answers

The questions we get most often — answered straight.

🚚 Where do you deliver?

Same-day delivery across Greater Colombo (Colombo, Nugegoda, Dehiwala, Rajagiriya, Battaramulla, Kotte). Next-day cold-chain delivery island-wide.

📦 What is the minimum order?

Retail orders have no minimum — order a single bottle if you like. Wholesale and restaurant accounts require a minimum of 48 bottles per order.

🧊 How long do bottles keep?

Sealed bottles last 21 days refrigerated at 4°C. Once opened, consume within 48 hours for peak flavour. Never freeze — it damages the cell structure of the raw juice.

🏪 Do you supply retail stores?

Yes — we supply select supermarkets, health stores, and cafés across Sri Lanka. Email wholesale@islandsqueeze.lk to discuss stocking us.

🌱 Are all products vegan?

All of our juices and blends are 100% plant-based and vegan. There are no animal-derived ingredients, additives, or processing aids used in any product.

🤝 Can I partner as a farmer?

We're always looking for ethical farms growing premium tropical fruits. Use the contact form above and select "Farm Partnership Enquiry" — our sourcing team will follow up within 3 business days.
