From Farm to Bottle

Every batch of Nano Banana juice is tracked from soil to consumer, ensuring absolute transparency, nutritional excellence, and premium quality.

Step 01

Fruit Selection

Only premium Grade-A fruits are handpicked from verified organic farms. Each fruit is checked for sugar contents (brix), weight, and skin integrity.

Step 02

Harvesting

Harvested at peak ripeness in the early morning to minimize sun exposure and heat stress on the fruit enzymes, locking in fresh flavor profiles.

Step 03

Cold Pressing

Gentle hydraulic press extraction. No high-speed blades or heat treatment is used, protecting fragile vitamins and cellular structures from oxidizing.

Step 04

Quality Testing

Every single batch is tested in our local Colombo laboratories. We verify nutritional density, check for impurities, and validate chemical-free status.

Step 05

Bottling

Juices are filled into eco-friendly, biodegradable, post-consumer recycled (PCR) containers under a strict sterile nitrogen-flushed atmosphere.

Step 06

Cold Chain Delivery

Shipped in customized temperature-controlled containers directly to metropolitan doorsteps within 24 hours of bottling, guaranteeing absolute peak freshness.

Sustainability Commitments

Preserving Sri Lanka's beautiful ecosystems is at the heart of our operations. Here is how we make a difference:

🌱

Sustainable Farming

Encouraging biodynamic polyculture models that rebuild depleted soils and avoid toxic chemical runoffs into forest systems.

♻️

Reduced Waste

Upcycling organic fruit husks and pulp residues into natural agricultural fertilizers and paper fibers, aiming for zero-landfill operations.

📦

Eco Packaging

Using 100% ocean-bound recycled plastic and biodegradable plant-based labels, minimizing standard carbon footprints.

🤝

Local Farmer Support

Paying up to 40% above fair-market averages. Providing modern irrigation tools and crop-preservation training for rural agricultural families.

💎

Ethical Sourcing

Ensuring 100% child-labor-free estates, offering safe working conditions, and promoting complete wage equality across all partnering plantations.
