The Full Journey

Every step, in detail.

From farm certification to your doorstep — nothing is left to chance.

Step 01

Farm Certification & Selection

Only farms meeting our strict ecological and ethical standards are onboarded. Each is visited personally twice a year. We grade fruit for brix (sugar content), weight, and skin integrity before any crop is accepted.

01
02
Step 02

Early Morning Harvest

Crops are harvested before 6am to minimise heat exposure. Cooler morning temperatures slow enzyme activity and oxidation, locking fresh-picked aromatics into each fruit before pressing.

Step 03

Cold-Chain Transport

Harvested fruit is loaded directly into refrigerated vehicles at 4°C and transported to our Colombo pressery within three hours. The cold chain is never broken from the moment of harvest.

03
04
Step 04

Hydraulic Cold Pressing

Our French-made hydraulic press applies 6,000 PSI of slow, steady pressure — no blades, no centrifuges, no heat. This preserves fragile vitamins, enzymes, and cellular antioxidants that high-speed methods destroy.

Step 05

HPP Sterilisation

Every batch undergoes High Pressure Processing — 87,000 PSI for 90 seconds inside a water chamber. This eliminates pathogens without heat, without additives, and without altering the fresh-pressed flavour profile.

05
06
Step 06

Laboratory Quality Test

Every single batch is tested in our Colombo-based laboratory for nutritional density, residual pesticides, microbial safety, and pH balance before a single bottle is filled. Batches that don't meet standards are rejected entirely.

Step 07

Nitrogen-Flush Bottling

Juice is filled into amber UV-blocking bottles under a hermetic nitrogen atmosphere, eliminating oxygen contact that causes oxidation, colour loss, and nutrient degradation. Caps are sealed and dated immediately.

07
08
Step 08

Chilled Island-Wide Delivery

Bottles are dispatched within 12 hours of pressing in temperature-controlled insulated packaging. Colombo orders arrive same-day chilled. Island-wide delivery is next-day, cold-chain throughout. Peak freshness, guaranteed.

Sustainability

Our Sustainability Standards

Protecting Sri Lanka's ecosystems is not a marketing claim for us — it is the operational foundation of every decision we make.

🌱

Regenerative Farming

We incentivise biodynamic polyculture models that actively rebuild depleted soils and prevent toxic runoff into forest and waterway ecosystems.

♻️

Zero-Waste Processing

Fruit pulp and husks are upcycled into natural agricultural fertilizer for our partner farms. Wastewater is treated on-site and recycled into irrigation systems.

📦

Eco Packaging

100% ocean-bound recycled PET bottles and plant-based adhesive labels. Our delivery packaging is compostable. We are targeting glass-only bottles by 2027.

☀️

Solar-Powered Pressery

Our Colombo facility runs on 80% solar-generated electricity via a 120-panel rooftop array installed in 2025. Full carbon neutrality planned by Q4 2026.

🤝

Fair Farmer Pricing

We pay 40% above prevailing market rates as a minimum floor. We also fund irrigation equipment, crop insurance, and seasonal training for all 47 partner farms.
