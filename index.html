Why Island Squeeze

Juice the way it should be.

We built everything from scratch to protect what actually matters — the nutrition, the farmers, and the island.

⚡

Hydraulic Cold Press

6,000 PSI of slow pressure — no blades, no centrifuges, no heat. Fragile enzymes and antioxidants are fully preserved from press to bottle.

🧊

Unbroken Cold Chain

Fruit enters refrigeration at 4°C at the moment of harvest and never leaves it. Temperature-controlled vehicles, pressery, and delivery packaging.

🔬

Lab-Tested Every Batch

Zero shortcuts. Every batch is tested for pesticide residue, microbial safety, pH, and nutritional density before a single bottle is filled.

🌿

100% Sri Lankan Fruit

Every ingredient is sourced from vetted partner farms across the island. Never imported. Never frozen. Always raw and in-season.

♻️

Zero-Waste Operations

Pulp becomes fertilizer. Wastewater becomes irrigation. Our facility runs on 80% solar. We're targeting full carbon neutrality by Q4 2026.

🤝

Fair Farmer Pay

Our 47 partner farms earn a minimum of 40% above market rates, plus irrigation equipment, crop insurance, and seasonal training from our team.

Our Story

Born from a single mango tree in Jaffna.

Island Squeeze started with one question: why does fresh fruit in Sri Lanka — some of the most extraordinary produce on earth — lose all its character by the time it reaches people in Colombo?

The answer was always the supply chain. Excessive heat, long transit, oxidation, and dilution. We built Island Squeeze to fix every single one of those problems from the ground up.

Today we partner with 47 smallholder farms across nine provinces, operate Sri Lanka's first cold-press HPP facility, and deliver to your door within 24 hours of harvest. Nothing hidden. Nothing added.
